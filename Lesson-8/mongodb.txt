MongoDB Benefits
	1. NoSQL (not only SQL)
	2. open Source
	3. Helps in Load Balancing
	4. Scalable
	5. robust
	6. flexible schema
	7. Support Sharding

Disadvantage
	1. fail in Complex Transaction
	2. not able to maintain ACID property
	3. Not support JOIN in write transaction (only support JOIN in READ)
